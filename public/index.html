Welcome to SceneJunction:

A Paradigm Shift in the World of Music Videos

Ever wished you could share your incredible concert footage with a community that appreciates it as much as you do? Welcome to SceneJunction, the platform that turns you from a fan into a contributor in the music industry.

Why SceneJunction?

We're not just another music website. We are a thriving ecosystem where acts, artists, and fans can upload, view, and even sell high-quality concert videos. What makes us unique? Your contributions directly support the artists you love, and you share in the profits too!

How It Works

  • Upload: Capture the energy of live performances and upload your videos in stunning HD.
  • Share: Share your videos with a community of creators and fans who appreciate quality music and artistry.
  • Stream: Enjoy an ad-free streaming experience, focused entirely on the content.
  • Monetize: Choose to sell your videos. When someone buys, the profits are shared between you, the artist, and SceneJunction.

Your Data, Your Control

Your privacy is important to us. Rest assured, your data is securely stored and handled in strict accordance with our privacy policy.
